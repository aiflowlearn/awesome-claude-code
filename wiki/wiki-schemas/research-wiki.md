# Research Wiki Schema

Use this schema for technical research, papers, and market knowledge.

```
wiki/
├── raw/
├── entities/      # papers, authors, systems, companies
├── concepts/      # methods, claims, mechanisms
├── evidence/      # quotes, metrics, experiments
└── syntheses/     # comparisons and conclusions
```

## Evidence Rules

- Keep claims linked to sources.
- Separate observation from interpretation.
- Record uncertainty explicitly.
- Prefer direct quotes for important claims.

## Synthesis Fields

- Question
- Short answer
- Supporting evidence
- Conflicting evidence
- Open questions
- Next sources to inspect
